Under the Hood

SecureAI implements a highly tuned **RandomForestClassifier** built inside the Scikit-learn environment. To resolve the extreme scarcity of fraud vectors, we leverage automated class reweighting adjustments.

By adjusting penalties proportionally to class frequencies, our algorithm maintains high **recall (sensitivity)**. This means the system flags anomalies even when they are disguised as standard consumer habits.
